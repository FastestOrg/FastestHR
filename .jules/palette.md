## 2024-05-06 - Password Visibility Toggles
**Learning:** Adding show/hide password visibility toggles significantly reduces user friction and blind typing errors, particularly in "reset password" or "create password" flows where users often struggle with complex password constraints.
**Action:** Always provide "show password" toggle buttons on password inputs (including confirm password fields) across all authentication components (Login, Register, ResetPassword) to improve accessibility and usability.
